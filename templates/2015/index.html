BREAKING NEWS

Shock news from astronomers who have today announced that they have detected a signal of extra terrestrial origin. The message, if that is what it is, is coming from the Northern Sky. The signal consists of a series of pulsed radio waves that the scientists claim cannot be of natural origin.

No translation of the signal has yet been made and as a result of this the astronomers will release their data in stages to registered members of the public at 10am on the 14th, 15th, 16th, 17th and 18th July 2014.
