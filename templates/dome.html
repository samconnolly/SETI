FREE Mobile Planetarium visits for local schools...

For details of all available booking dates and the information you will need to put in your email to go on the waiting list before making a formal booking is on the Bookings page.

Please note we do not do shows for Reception and Year 1 classes.

Since June 2011 the University of Southampton has done shows at over 80 schools for free and done outreach and public engagement with over over 20,000 people!

Our aim is inspire children and adults (young and old) to study/take an interest in Physics and Astronomy.

We are offering schools in the local area the chance to receive a visit for FREE !

Schools (catering for Primary-A-level) and colleges (catering for AS/A-Levels) must be within a 1.5 hour travel limit of the University of Southampton. To see avaliability this term go to the Bookings page.

We must reach at least 60 students in one visit, from a minimum of 3 x 45 minute shows. Ideally we would like to see at least 130 students in one day, from 5 shows assuming 26 students in each show !

Our absolute limit for a day is 6 x 45- 50 minute shows!

Even for primary schools we insist on 45 minute slots- because the students have so many questions :)

Teachers, please do not forget to timetable a break for us to have LUNCH- we always appreciate a FREE lunch :)

The Teacher is responsible for behaviour management while we are inside the dome. Please do NOT leave us alone with the students. Please make sure there is always at least one teacher inside the dome with us.
